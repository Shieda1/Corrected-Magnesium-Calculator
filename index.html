What is the corrected magnesium calculator?
The corrected magnesium calculator is a tool that computes the corrected magnesium level in a patient with low albumin.
How to calculate corrected magnesium?
To calculate corrected magnesium you should use the following formula:
corrected magnesium = patients magnesium + 0.005 * (40 - patients albumin)
